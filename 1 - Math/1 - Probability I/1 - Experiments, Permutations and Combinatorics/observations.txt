Put the Formula About Permutations and Combinatorics with and without Replacements

\ Source: https://towardsdatascience.com/how-to-think-about-combinatorics-like-a-data-scientist-bddbd18eff80

- Permutations with Replacement: 
			n^r
- Permutations wihout Replacement:
			n! / (n-r)!

- Combinatorics without Replacement:
			n! / ((n-r)! * r!)

- Combinatorics with Replacement:
			(n+r-1)! / (n-1)! * r!
